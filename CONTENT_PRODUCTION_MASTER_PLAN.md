✅ Smartphone with HD camera (1080p+)

✅ USB microphone (30-50₪)

✅ Blue background
✅ Simple graphics/text overlays

Best:
✅ Professional camera (even old DSLR)
✅ Ring light
✅ Proper microphone
✅ Graphics with animation
✅ Professional editing
```

**Editing Software:**
```
Free Options:
✅ OBS Studio (open source)
✅ DaVinci Resolve (free version)
✅ HitFilm Express
✅ CapCut (mobile)

Paid:
✅ Adobe Premiere Pro
✅ Final Cut Pro
✅ Vegas Pro
```

**Recording Setup:**
```
1. Screen Recording:
   - Use OBS Studio (free)
   - Record at 1080p 60fps
   - Good microphone audio
   - Clear audio narration

2. Interview/Talking Head:
   - Phone on tripod
   - Face lit properly
   - Quiet room
   - Good microphone
   - Clean background

3. Graphics:
   - Use Canva (free)
   - Or hire on Fiverr (10-30₪/video)
   - Keep it simple
   - Brand consistent
```

**Quality Checklist:**
```
☑️ Audio clear (no background noise)
☑️ Video smooth (no shaking)
☑️ Text readable (large enough)
☑️ Colors consistent (brand colors)
☑️ Pacing good (not too fast, not too slow)
☑️ Hebrew subtitles (for international reach)
☑️ Call to action clear
☑️ Links in description
☑️ Engaging thumbnail
