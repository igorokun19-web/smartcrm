## 📞 DEMO CALL TALKING POINTS

When someone requests a demo, you'll want to:

1. **Listen (3 min):** "What brings you in? What's your biggest challenge?"
2. **Show (7 min):**
   - Dashboard overview
   - Add a lead live (show scoring)
   - Show pipeline
   - Show WhatsApp integration
3. **Quick Win (3 min):** "Here's what you could do this week..."
4. **Close (2 min):** "Ready to try it?"

**Objection handlers:**
- "This is just for big teams" → "No, it's for solo pros and teams"
- "I already tried CRM" → "This was built specifically for service pros"
- "It's too expensive" → "It's free forever, actually"
- "I don't have time" → "Takes 5 minutes, and saves you 10 hours/week"
