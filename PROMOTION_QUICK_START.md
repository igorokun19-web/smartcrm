## 🛑 Common Mistakes to AVOID

❌ Don't spam the same message everywhere  
✅ Tailor each platform (LinkedIn ≠ WhatsApp)

❌ Don't only focus on sales  
✅ Share value first, sell second (80/20 rule)

❌ Don't disappear after first week  
✅ Consistency beats intensity

❌ Don't ignore comments/DMs  
✅ Reply within 24 hours

❌ Don't use automated tools (yet)  
✅ Build relationships manually first

## ⚡ Pro Tips for Success

1. **Use Your Story**
   - You built this for YOUR business
   - Share your personal journey
   - People buy from people, not bots

2. **Focus on Problems First**
   - Don't start with "buy this"
   - Start with "are you frustrated with..."
   - Then show your solution

3. **Ask for Feedback**
   - Every interaction = learning
   - Use feedback to improve
   - Show people you listen

4. **Create FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)**
   - "Only free for first 100"
   - "Early bird discount"
   - "Limited-time offer"

5. **Be Authentic**
   - No fake testimonials
   - Share real numbers
   - Admit what you're not good at yet
