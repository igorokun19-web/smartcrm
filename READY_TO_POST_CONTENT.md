Every lead is NOT created equal.

Some are ready to buy RIGHT NOW (hot 🔥)
Some are interested but not ready (warm 🟡)
Some are just researching (cold ❄️)

Most service businesses treat all leads the same... and waste time chasing cold ones.

That's why we built SmartCRM's Lead Scoring System:

🔴 0-25 pts: Initial contact (just started talking)
🟡 25-50 pts: Interested (responding to messages)

• Lead status (new → quoted → won)
