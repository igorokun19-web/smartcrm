## 🎵 Next: Add Music

You have THREE options:

### 🥇 Option 1: EASIEST - Kapwing (Online, 5 min)
1. Go to: https://kapwing.com/studio
2. Upload your video
3. Click "Add" → "Audio"
4. Pick royalty-free music
5. Export with music
✅ No downloads needed
✅ Free tier works great
⏱️ 5 minutes

### 🥈 Option 2: CapCut (Desktop App, 10 min)
1. Download: https://www.capcut.com/desktop
2. Open CapCut
3. Import video
4. "Audio" tab → Add music
5. Export
✅ Free desktop app
✅ Professional features
⏱️ 10 minutes

### 🥉 Option 3: Command Line (Advanced)
1. Install ffmpeg: `choco install ffmpeg`
2. Get music file: `background_music.mp3`
3. Run: `python add_music_moviepy.py`
✅ Fully automated
⏱️ 15 minutes
