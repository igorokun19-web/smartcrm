## ⚡ PRODUCTIVITY HACKS

**To stay consistent and sane:**

1. **Batch Content Creation:**
   - Spend 1 hour Sunday creating all week's main posts
   - Write them in a document
   - Schedule them in bulk

2. **Use Templates:**
   - Have 5 post templates you use repeatedly
   - Just change the numbers/example
   - Saves tons of time

3. **Automation (Optional, Week 3+):**
   - Use Buffer or Later to schedule posts
   - Can schedule weeks in advance
   - Frees up time for outreach

4. **Quick Wins:**
   - Comment on competitors' posts (2 min) = easy engagement
   - Retweet + add your comment = 1 min, high reach
   - Share others' content = builds relationships

5. **Priority Matrix:**
   - URGENT & IMPORTANT: Cold DMs, demo calls, customer follow-up
   - IMPORTANT: Content creation, engagement
   - URGENT: Respond to comments
   - NOT URGENT: Creative stuff
