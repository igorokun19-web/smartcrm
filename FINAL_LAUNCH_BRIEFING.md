## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

**Tip 1: Batch Your Content Creation**
- Spend 1 hour Sunday creating all week's posts
- Schedule them for posting throughout week
- Saves time, maintains consistency

**Tip 2: Use Templates**
- Have 5 post templates you reuse
- Just change examples/numbers
- Faster than creating new each time

**Tip 3: Track Everything**
- Create simple Google Sheet
- Note: date, platform, engagement, website visitors, leads
- Review weekly to see what works

**Tip 4: Speed Kills Objections**
- Reply within 2 hours = 7x better results
- Schedule demo same day = higher close rate
- Follow up within 24 hours = not forgotten

**Tip 5: Focus on Platforms That Work**
- Week 1-2: Try all platforms
- Week 3+: Double down on best 2
- Don't spread thin across all 5
