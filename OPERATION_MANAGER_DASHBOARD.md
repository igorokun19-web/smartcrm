## 📞 INQUIRY PIPELINE MANAGEMENT

### When an inquiry comes in:

```
IMMEDIATE (0-30 min):
1. Send automated response (thank you, will follow up)
2. Personally read the message
3. Qualify: Are they B2B/service business?

QUICK (30 min - 2 hours):
1. Reply with personalized message
2. Address their specific pain point
3. Invite to free trial or demo call

FOLLOW-UP (24 hours):
1. If no response, send second message
2. Offer specific solution/help
3. Build relationship

CONVERSION (3-7 days):
1. Schedule demo call if interested
2. Show them how CRM helps THEIR business
3. Close trial signup or paid

RETENTION (ongoing):
1. Weekly check-in with active users
2. Support and training
3. Upgrade/upsell based on needs
```
